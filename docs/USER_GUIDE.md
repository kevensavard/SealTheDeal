## 🚀 Getting Started

### Account Setup

1. **Sign Up**: Visit our landing page and click "Get Started"
2. **Complete Profile**: Add your name, email, and company information
3. **Verify Email**: Check your email for verification link
4. **Choose Plan**: Start with our free plan or upgrade to Pro

## 📝 Creating Your First Contract

### Step 1: Access Contract Creation

- Click "Create Contract" from the dashboard
- Or use the "+" button in the navigation
- Select "New Contract" from the quick actions

### Step 2: Fill Contract Details

#### Required Information
- **Contract Type**: Choose from Freelance, Car Sale, Property Rental, Equipment Lease, or Custom
- **Parties Involved**: Add at least two parties (names and contact info)
- **Description**: Detailed description of the contract purpose

#### Optional Information
- **Contract Title**: Leave empty for AI-generated title
- **Payment Terms**: Specify amounts, schedules, and methods
- **Deadline/Duration**: Set contract expiration dates
- **Special Clauses**: Add custom terms and conditions

#### Type-Specific Fields

**Car Sale Contracts:**
- Make, Model, Year
- Mileage and unit (miles/km)
- VIN number
- Vehicle condition

**Property Rental Contracts:**
- Property address
- Property type
- Rent amount and period
- Security deposit

**Equipment Lease Contracts:**
- Equipment description
- Serial number
- Lease term and amount

### Step 3: Generate with AI

1. Click "Generate with AI" button
2. Watch the premium animation as your contract is created
3. Review the generated content in real-time
4. Make adjustments if needed

### Step 4: Save and Export

- **Save**: Store the contract in your dashboard
- **Download PDF**: Export as professional PDF
- **Share**: Send via email (coming soon)

## 📋 Using Templates

## 👥 Managing Clients

### Adding Clients

1. Go to the Clients section
2. Click "Add Client" button
3. Fill in client information:
   - First and Last Name (required)
   - Email (required)
   - Company (optional)
   - Phone (optional)
   - Address (optional)
   - Notes (optional)

### Client Actions

**View Client Details:**
- Click on any client card
- View contact information and contract history

**Edit Client:**
- Click the edit icon on client card
- Update information and save changes

**Use for Contract:**
- Click "Use for Contract" button
- Automatically fills party information in new contracts

**Delete Client:**
- Click delete icon and confirm
- Note: This won't affect existing contracts

### Client Search and Filter

- Use the search bar to find clients by name
- Filter by company using the dropdown
- Sort by name, company, or creation date

## 📊 Contract Management

### Contract Status

Track your contracts through different stages:

- **Draft**: Initial creation, not yet sent
- **Sent**: Contract sent to parties for review
- **Signed**: Contract signed by all parties
- **Expired**: Contract past expiration date
- **Cancelled**: Contract cancelled by parties

### Contract Actions

**Edit Contract:**
- Click edit icon on contract card
- Modify title, content, status, or expiration
- Save changes

**Change Status:**
- Use the status dropdown on contract card
- Update to reflect current stage
- Automatic notifications sent to relevant parties

**Download PDF:**
- Click download icon
- Contract exported as professional PDF
- Includes all formatting and signatures

**Delete Contract:**
- Click delete icon and confirm
- Permanently removes contract (use with caution)

### Contract Search and Filter

- Search contracts by title or content
- Filter by status, type, or date range
- Sort by creation date, status, or expiration

## 🔒 Data Protection

### Export Your Data

SealTheDeal is GDPR-compliant and provides data export:

1. Go to Settings > Data Protection
2. Click "Export My Data"
3. Download complete data package including:
   - Personal information
   - All contracts and content
   - Client information
   - Activity history
   - Account settings

### Data Retention

- **Active Account**: Data retained while account is active
- **Deleted Account**: Data deleted within 30 days
- **Backup Retention**: Encrypted backups kept for 90 days

### Privacy Controls

- Control what information is shared
- Manage notification preferences
